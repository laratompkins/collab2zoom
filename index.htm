Major Differences between Zoom and Collaborate

Collaborate only runs in the browser

Collaborate Zoom
Browser-based web conferencing platforms have poor performance for students on slow(er) internet connections. Zoom provides apps for most operating systems and devices.

Collaborate Zoom
Student time in session can be sent to the Grade Center to automate attendance points. Reporting available on Zoom website

Recording Retention Policies

Collaborate Zoom
Recordings are removed by IT 2 weeks after the term ends that they were created in. Recordings automatically expire after 30 days.
*In progress: Zoom recording integration with YuJa

Collaborate Zoom
Always-on course room by default. Students can use any time. Sessions without moderator can be manually configured but toolset is limited.
